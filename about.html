Our Legacy

S.G.Balekundri Institute of Technology was established in the year 2008, by Shri Siddhharameshwar Education Trust. It is located in the heart of Belagavi city, Karnataka.

Within ten years of its establishment, the college has entrenched itself in the league of the most reputed institutes of Belagavi. The institution is known for academic rigor, vibrant campus life, and strong industry connections.

Campus & Infrastructure

The college campus is spread over 10 acres of land, in the midst of an educational locality, flanked by a number of reputed educational institutes. Highly developed infrastructure for the entire engineering course is available, including:

  • State-of-the-art laboratories
  • Digital classrooms & innovation center
  • Central library with digital resources
  • High-speed campus-wide connectivity
  • Sports arena & recreation zones

Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in engineering education, fostering innovation, research, and ethical leaders who contribute to global technological advancement and societal well-being.

Mission

• Provide quality technical education with strong industry interface.
• Nurture creativity, critical thinking, and entrepreneurial spirit.
• Instill values of integrity, teamwork, and lifelong learning.
• Bridge the gap between academia and industry demands.
